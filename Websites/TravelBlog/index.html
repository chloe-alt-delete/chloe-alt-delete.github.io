Traveling to Italy has arguably been one of the best places that I've ever been to. The food, the atmosphere, and all the culture there is definietly lives up to it's name. During my travels, I got to explore many places with ancient history tied with them. I've even been to the ancient civilization destroyed to ash, Pompeii. Personally, I would go again. I had a really great time there and it was fun to walk around and see the sights.

Tip! Always eat pizza with a fork and knife in Italy.


Destinations


Rome

Rome, the capital of Italy, has been around for over two millenia. There are many things to do in the city such as sight seeing, going to eat in nice restaurants, and guided tours.


Pompeii

The city of ash can still be explored today. If you're interested in the spooky side of it, ghost tours of the city are available! If that's not your style, then you can walk around and enjoy the sights that Pompeii has to offer.


Almafi Coast

The Almafi Coast is known for its quaint villages. There are a lot of things to do there. You can take a boat tour, go shopping, or even eat lemon sorbet out of a lemon the size of your head! They are the best. 10/10 would reccomend.
